The Problem

The system

is broken.

Millions of Indian citizens are entitled to welfare benefits they will never receive. Not because the schemes don't exist — they do. 1,247 of them.

A farmer in rural UP doesn't know he qualifies for PM Kisan and Fasal Bima and Kisan Credit Card — simultaneously. A young woman in Bihar is entitled to 7 different schemes just by virtue of her age and address.

The information is scattered across 40+ government portals, buried in bureaucratic PDFs, written in language nobody actually speaks.
